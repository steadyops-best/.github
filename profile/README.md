# SteadyOps

SteadyOps publishes practical DevOps/SRE resources for teams that need safer production systems, clearer runbooks and more predictable operations.

The public repositories here are small, reusable checklists and templates. They are written for engineers who need to make production work safer without adding unnecessary process.

## Focus areas

- Production reliability and incident readiness
- Zero-downtime deployment checks
- Kubernetes rollback and production readiness
- PostgreSQL high availability and backup/restore review
- Monitoring, alerting and operational visibility
- Disaster recovery runbooks
- Nginx, Docker, systemd and Linux operations
- Infrastructure audits and automation

## How to use these repositories

Copy the relevant checklist into your release ticket, incident runbook or internal documentation system. Adjust thresholds, owners, commands and validation steps to match your production environment.

These resources are intentionally practical. They avoid fake benchmarks and broad claims; the value is in making operational decisions explicit before production pressure starts.
